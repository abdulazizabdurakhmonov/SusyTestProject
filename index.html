How to use Clorox® for Black and Dark Clothes?

Pre-Wash Apply Clorox for Black and Dark Clothes directly to stain, rub the cloth gently, let stand 5 minutes and wash normally. Do not allow product to dry on fabric. Clorox for Black and Dark Clothes is also effective on dried in stains

In-Wash Add 100ml (2 caps) of Clorox© Clothes with your favorite detergent, and start your normal wash cycle. Clorox© Clothes will remove your toughest stains and boost the colors of your clothes.

Soaking Add 10ml (2 teaspoons) of Clorox© Clothes to each 1L of water with your favorite detergent. Soak for 5 minutes then wash as usual. For tough stains, soak for 1 hour. Clorox© Clothes will enhance the detergent effectiveness and powerfully remove dried in stains making your garments look impeccably clean and bright.
